About the Course

This course prepares graduates with a valuable blend of hardware and software skills essential for the rapidly evolving fields of mobile systems and the Internet of Things (IoT), spanning from sensors and electronic devices to networked applications and embedded systems.
Students gain expertise in embedding computers into products and developing essential software drivers, with advanced studies in the final year focusing on IoT technologies and optimisation techniques for specialized applications such as life-sustaining medical devices and real-time environmental monitoring systems.
Practical learning, comprising 50% of course time, is emphasized through hands-on assignments and project work in modern, well-equipped labs.
Industry partnerships with leading companies such as Intel, IBM, and Cisco further enhance career prospects, preparing graduates to meet the demands of technology-driven industries.
